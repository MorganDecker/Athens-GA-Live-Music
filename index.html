The man behind the camera

Gregory Frederick

If you've been to many shows around Athens, odds are you've seen Gregory there filming. He's a husband, father, and retired teacher with an addiction to live music. Focusing on local acts for the past 5 years, he seeks to document as much music as humanly possible. He was recently awarded a grant from the Athens Area Art Council, enabling equipment upgrades. Video and audio quality will be as sharp and clear as the live setting it was recorded in. Artists' rights and creativity will always be paramount. Artists can be sure that they will be well respresented on camera, and will have something that they can share for decades to come.
